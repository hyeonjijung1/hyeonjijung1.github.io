About

I’m an ECE student who loves building hardware that ends up in the lab and in the world. I gravitate to FPGA/RTL, careful PCB layout, and measurements that match the waveform I simulated. Calm bring-up, clean docs, and test benches you can rerun a month later make me happy.

Outside circuits, I like leading teams and creating spaces where people grow. I mentor younger students, help organize events, and I’m drawn to projects with a sustainability heartbeat.

Recent work: STM32WL LoRa telemetry (custom packet/CRC, RF validation), and an FPGA audio/UX stack with real-time I/O.
