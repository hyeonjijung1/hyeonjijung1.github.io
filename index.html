About

I’m an ECE student who loves building hardware that ends up in the lab and in the world. My favorite days blend FPGA/RTL, careful PCB layout, and scope captures that match simulation. I care about calm bring-up, clean documentation, and test benches you can rerun a month later.

Beyond circuits, I like leading teams and creating spaces that help people grow. I mentor younger students, organize events, and I’m drawn to projects with a sustainability heartbeat.

Recent work: a STM32WL LoRa telemetry system with custom packet/CRC and RF validation, and an FPGA audio/UX stack with real-time I/O.
